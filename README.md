## Target Company Research: The "Federer" Hunt
DeepThought Business Analytics Internship Assignment

## 🎯 Project Overview
This repository contains a data-driven research project focused on identifying and qualifying "Federer Companies"—high-growth, technical, promoter-led manufacturing MSMEs in India.

The goal was to move beyond generic company lists and find specialty manufacturers that operate with scientific rigor, making them ideal candidates for DeepThought’s Scientific Execution framework.

## 🏗️ The "Federer" Framework
In this project, companies were evaluated against six specific criteria to determine their "Federer Score" (0-100):

C1: Manufacturer (Physical production vs. trading/services)

C2: India-based (Local promoter-driven vs. multinational subsidiary)

C3: Differentiated (Patents, R&D, and technical moats)

C4: Technical Decision-Maker (PhD/IIT/Scientist-led leadership)

C5: Sector Tailwind (PLI Schemes, China+1, and high-growth segments)

C6: Active Growth Signals (Hiring, capacity expansion, and certifications)

## 🔬 Research Methodology
My research focused on the Hyderabad Hub within the Specialty Biotech & Chemicals segment.

Primary Source: Cross-referenced the DSIR (Department of Scientific & Industrial Research) directory to verify R&D units.

Cluster Mapping: Targeted specific zones like Genome Valley and IDA Pashamylaram.

Financial Scrutiny: Used Zauba Corp/Tofler to ensure revenue fell within the ₹50Cr–₹500Cr sweet spot.

Quality Control: Applied a strict "Scientific Execution" filter to disqualify PE-owned or commodity-focused firms.

Data Processing: Microsoft Excel.

Logic Partner: AI (Gemini/ChatGPT) for technical segment classification.
